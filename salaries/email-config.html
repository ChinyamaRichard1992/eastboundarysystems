Email Configuration

Configure SMTP settings for OTP delivery

Important: Email configuration is required for OTP verification to work. Without proper email settings, users cannot complete registration.

Gmail SMTP Setup Instructions

  1. Go to your Google Account settings
  2. Enable 2-Factor Authentication (if not already enabled)
  3. Go to Security → App Passwords
  4. Select App: Mail, Device: Other (Custom name)
  5. Name it: East Boundary Systems
  6. Google will generate a 16-character password
  7. Copy that password and paste it in the "SMTP Password" field below
  8. Enter your Gmail address in the "SMTP Email" field
