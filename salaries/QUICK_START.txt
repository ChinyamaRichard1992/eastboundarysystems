🚀 HOW TO START:

STEP 1: Open the Login Page
   Navigate to: http://localhost/eastboundarysystems/salaries/auth.html

STEP 2: Create Your Account
   - Click "Sign Up" tab
   - Fill in your name, email, role, password
   - Click "Create Account"
   - ✅ Account created and saved permanently!

STEP 3: Login
   - Enter your email and password
   - Check "Remember Me" (optional)
   - Click "Login"
   - 🎉 You're redirected to the payroll system

STEP 4: Use the System
   - Add employees
   - Process payroll
   - Generate payslips
   - Send emails
   - All data is automatically saved!

STEP 5: Close Browser & Refresh Test
   - Close your browser completely
   - Open it again
   - Go to: http://localhost/eastboundarysystems/salaries/index.html
   - ✅ You're still logged in!
   - ✅ All your data is still there!
